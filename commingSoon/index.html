1. Pioneers in Premium Quality

This is what makes Austin one of the best plywood manufacturing companies in India.

Austin benefits from its state-of-the-art facilities in Kolkata, Guwahati, and the newly inaugurated Lucknow plant. Its vast production capacity of 4.50 million sheets per year ensures a plentiful & consistent supply of different types & categories of plywood without compromising quality. This scale of production makes them a company providing one of the best plywood in India for projects of any scale.

3. Trusted for Innovation and Certifications

“Evolving with Market Demands and Global Standards”

Austin Plywood has been quick to adapt to market needs, earning an E-0 certification for eco-friendly emissions, positioning the brand as one of the leaders in producing plywood that’s sustainable. In the Indian market, having gained a strong foothold, Austin's focus on adhering to global standards reflects its forward-thinking approach. This seeps into their commitment to producing products that don't add to the planet's carbon footprint. Without consideration for the ecosystem, even plywood of the best quality in India holds little value.

4. Strategic Presence Across India

“Serving 12 Major Cities with Integrated Solutions”

No matter how good the quality of your product is, without distribution, business would never exist in the first place. Robust sales and service networks across 12 major cities help Austin become a major player in producing the best ply suitable for all types of furniture. FAQs

1. What makes Austin Plywood stand out from other brands?

Austin Plywood is the only Indian brand certified by Lloyd’s Register with BS-1088 approval, ensuring unmatched quality, reliability, and water-proof properties.

2. How does Austin Plywood support sustainable practices?

Austin Plywood holds an E-0 certification, indicating its commitment to eco-friendly production, reducing harmful emissions, and supporting sustainable interior solutions.

3. What warranties does Austin Plywood offer?

Austin provides industry-leading warranties like a 500% Lifetime Warranty on Club Plus and a 200% money-back guarantee that extends to 31 years on Defender 2X.

4. How do I choose the right plywood for furniture?

Consider factors like durability, water resistance, and intended use. Austin Plywood's range includes options like Platinum Plus for luxury furniture and Defender 2X for fire safety.

5. What are the different grades of plywood?

Common plywood grades include BWP (Boiling Water Proof), MR (Moisture Resistant), and fire-retardant. 

6. Which brands are recommended for plywood in India?

Austin Plywood stands out for its BS-1088 certification, 500% Lifetime Warranty, and innovative, sustainable products, making it a trusted choice for Indian households.
