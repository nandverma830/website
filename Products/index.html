Our Plywood Range For Your Furniture

Our Blockboard Selection For Your Spaces

YantraPly blockboard is a popular choice for construction and interior design, offering remarkable strength and stability. Composed of a core made of wooden blocks sandwiched between layers of plywood or veneer, this termite-proof, bwp and fire-resistant blockboard is renowned for its resistance to warping and cracking. Its durability and versatility make it a preferred option for projects where structural integrity is paramount.

Our Flush Door Choice For Your Interiors

YantraPly Flush doors in modern architecture and interior design, grace many homes and commercial spaces with their sleek appearance and functionality. Constructed with a solid core, flush doors boast a smooth surface that adds a touch of sophistication to any interior. Their clean lines and contemporary aesthetic make it a popular choice for interior doorways in residences, offices, and commercial establishments. These termite-proof flush doors not only enhance the visual appeal of a space but also offer durability and ease of maintenance, making them a practical choice.

Our WPC Choice For Your Interiors

Made from recycled wood and plastic with zero formaldehyde or harmful emissions, YantraPly's WPC boards are a top choice for eco-conscious builders. These WPC Solid Door frames at the best price offer design flexibility for doors, windows, furniture, and more.

YantraPly WPC Board

Description

Wood Plastic Composites (WPC) is a modern material that can be used in numerous potential applications. WPC board is a long-lasting, environment-friendly, durable, and extremely high water and moisture resistant material. It is a blend of wood fiber/wood flour and thermoplastics and can also be made from inorganic fillers and plastic composites. YantraPly’s WPC board has gained immense popularity and the faiths of customers, architects and builders, those who have experienced its long list of benefits.

WPC Solid Door

Description

WPC doors are aesthetically pleasing, affordable and low maintenance. They do not require surface protection, which eliminates the need to coat the surface, reducing the time and cost spent on the surface coating. WPC doors are anti-corrosion, anti-aging and its antibacterial properties lead to 0% warping and splintering of the door. It has a uniform thickness without undulation, bending and has a smooth surface for lamination. It offers dimensional accuracy and stability in varying humidity ensuring strength and elegance.
