About Us:

Founded by Mr. Mahavir Ji, YantraPly is one of the leading players in the interior infrastructure industry. Celebrating a remarkable 18-year journey since 2006, YantraPly Plywood continues to be a hallmark of product excellence and streamlined service in this industry. Commencing its journey from Kolkata, YantraPly established its first manufacturing facility at Delhi. The humble beginning soon gave way to major expansion, as the company set up its second factory in Siwani. In 2022, YantraPly commissioned one of India’s largest plywood manufacturing facilities in Delhi.

Today, YantraPly operates with a total installed capacity of 4.50 million sheets per annum. To offer best-in-class sales service, we have a well-equipped corporate office in Kolkata. Our corporate office serves as the hub, complemented by marketing offices strategically located in 12 major cities. This network brings a personal touch and integrated service to our customers. At YantraPly Plywood, we are committed to delivering products and solutions that represent an invaluable investment for our customers, along with lifetime insurance.

BRAND STORY

YantraPly was rewarded by customer preference for its outstanding quality; it became the only brand in the country to be approved by Lloyds Register and was certified as a BS-1088 product

Founded by father son duo of Mr. Surendra Agarwal and Mr. Nishant Agarwal in the year 2006, YantraPly started with the objective of fulfilling the needs of the discerning user for high quality premium plywood.

The market was flush with popular brands who were manufacturing and supplying ordinary quality plywood in the name of water proof category. YantraPly changed the landscape and was immediately rewarded by customer preference for its outstanding quality, it became the only brand in the country to be approved by Lloyds Register and was certified as a BS-1088 product.

Continuing on its path to deliver world class products with super-fast service YantraPly soon became the favourite of all users. While continuing to cater efficiently to its customers YantraPly built a strong and committed network to cater to these customers. With time YantraPly developed a strong bond with its network partners and continued to be showered by the professional support of Interior Designers and wood craftsmen. With time, fame of the YantraPly quality standards traversed the length and breadth of the country and it has become the favorite plywood of all discerning users throughout the country. Realizing the need of the consumer for high quality plywood YantraPly increased its capacity manifold stage wise and today has a total installed capacity of 4.50 million sheets PA. Brand YantraPly has been rising like a unicorn and is best placed to take a leap into the list of top plywood brands in the country. To enable this journey YantraPly has in place a team of dedicated and passionate professionals assembled from the best background and tradecraft.
The story shall continue……
